How to buy and sell

Step-by-step guide to minting and redeeming ALP tokens on BNB Smart Chain.

Buying ALP (Mint)

To get ALP tokens, you send BNB to the smart contract. The contract calculates how many ALP you receive based on the current index price.

1
Connect your wallet
Click “Connect Wallet” on the main page. You need MetaMask or any other wallet compatible with BNB Smart Chain.
2
Enter the BNB amount
In the “Buy ALP” section, type how much BNB you want to spend. You’ll see a preview with the fee and how many ALP you’ll receive.
3
Confirm the transaction
Press “Buy ALP” and confirm in your wallet. The BNB is sent to the contract and you receive ALP instantly.
4
Check your balance
Your ALP balance will appear updated in the top bar and you can verify it on BscScan.
Mint formula
alpAmount = (netBnb × bnbUsdPrice) / indexPrice

Selling ALP (Redeem)

To get your BNB back, you burn ALP tokens and the contract returns the equivalent BNB based on the index value.

1
Enter the ALP amount
In the “Sell ALP” section, type how many ALP you want to sell. Use the MAX button to sell your entire balance.
2
Confirm and receive BNB
Press “Sell ALP” and confirm. Your ALP are burned and you receive BNB directly in your wallet.
Redeem formula
grossBnb = (alpAmount × indexPrice) / bnbUsdPrice
netBnb = grossBnb - fee (0.30%)

Fees

0.30% per operation (both mint and redeem). The fee is automatically deducted before calculating the tokens or BNB you receive. Additionally, you’ll pay BNB Chain gas (usually < $0.05 per transaction).

Prerequisites

  • An EVM-compatible wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Rabby, etc.).
  • BNB in your wallet to pay gas and to mint.
  • Be connected to BNB Smart Chain (chainId 56). The app will help you switch networks if needed.
