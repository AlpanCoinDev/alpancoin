AlpanCoin — What is it?

An index token that tracks the average price of the top 50 cryptocurrencies by market cap.

The concept

AlpanCoin (ALP) is an ERC-20 token on BNB Smart Chain that works as a decentralized index fund. Instead of buying 50 cryptocurrencies separately, ALP gives you exposure to all of them in a single token.

ALP index price = simple average of the USD price of all 50 assets in the index. Prices are updated via Chainlink Price Feeds (on-chain, real-time) and an automated keeper.

Index components

The index includes the top 50 assets by market cap: BTC, ETH, BNB, SOL, XRP, DOGE, ADA, AVAX, LINK, DOT, and 40 more. The full list with live prices is available on the main page.

Key features

  • Mint: send BNB and receive ALP at the current index price.
  • Redeem: burn ALP and get back BNB proportional to the index value.
  • Fee: 0.30% per operation (mint and redeem).
  • Max supply: 21,000,000 ALP.
  • Non-custodial: contracts are public and verifiable on BscScan.
